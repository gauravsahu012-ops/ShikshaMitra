What is the validity of the Category Certificate (SC / ST / OBC / EWS-Gen.) and PwD certificate?

1. Certificate of category (SC/ST/OBC/MBC/ EWS/TSP), issued by the competent authority (if applicable). In case of OBC, certificate should not be issued before 01/09/2023. Grace period of certificate is admissible ( which is two years) with an undertaking.
2. Undertaking by OBC/MBC in required format (for non-creamy layer), (in case of grace period).
3. Certificate for Persons with Disabilities (PwD), (if applicable).
4. Certificate for Ex-Service men, (if applicable).To obtain the most accurate information about the validity period of an OBC certificate, it is recommended to consult the issuing authority or refer to the official government guidelines for OBC reservations and certificates in your region.(for more details refer REAP booklet 2024)

Procedure for Filling Online Application cum Registration and College Choice Form?

Step-1: Submission of Application cum Registration Form fee.
Step-2: Filling of online Application cum registration form and College Choice form.
Step-3: Candidates will receive confirmation E-mail/ message at registered E-mail ID/ Mobile number after successful submission of Application cum Registration form and College Choice form.
Step-4: Candidates must take Printouts/ Hardcopy only after final submission of online application form and college choice form. Note: - The hardcopy of application form is not required to send/ submit to the REAP-2024 office. Duplicate/Multiple Registrations are not permitted in REAP-24. Candidates are advised to visit the REAP-2024 web portal regularly for latest information. (details are given in booklet page number 11-12)

My registration fees is deducted but registration not done.

Please check your transaction details on the "Check Transaction Status" link on the candidate control panel using your temporary transaction number. After some time, you will receive your permanent number on your registered email ID also check spam box.

If any candidate faces any problem in the remittance of fee due to a failed transaction, what can he/she do?

If the payment status of an online transaction is not communicated by the payment gateway in real time then fee payment is not completed and the candidate will not be able to download the Acknowledgement page. In this case, the candidate is required to pay the fee again through online mode and download the acknowledgment page. The amount due to the failed transaction of online mode will be refunded.
